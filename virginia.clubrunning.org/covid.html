COVID-19 Response

Practicing in-person safely & responsibly

Club Running is working to bring runners together during these isolating times. We're moving forward with practice, emphasizing safety and responsibility.


In-Person Practice

Spring 2021 in-person practice begins on Monday, February 15. We're making significant modifications to our typical practice plan to make sure our members are safe.

  • Schedule. We'll hold official practices twice daily Monday-Friday. Morning practice starts at 8:00am and afternoon practice starts at 4:00pm. We'll meet at Nameless Field.

  • Spreadsheet. You can sign up to attend in-person practice through that week's spreadsheet. Make sure you're on our mailing list to receive the spreadsheet. If you missed this week's spreadsheet, email our webmaster.

  • Symptoms. Do not attend practice if you are experiencing symptoms consistent with COVID or have been in close contact with a COVID-positive person. Refer to University guidance regarding appropriate quarantine protocol.

  • Masks. Masks are required before, during, and after your runs at practice. If you don't have a suitable running mask, we'll provide one. Email our secretary to get yours for free.

  • Group size. In compliance with the University gathering limit, make sure you're running in groups of six people or fewer. It's okay if more than six people show up to practice, but it's important that you quickly divide into appropriately sized groups.

  • Observe social distancing before runs, during runs, and after them. Stay six feet apart from others whenever possible.

  • Contact tracing. While we can't require you to, we hope you'll let our president know if you or someone very close to you contracts COVID-19. That way, we can notify your recent running partners and encourage them to self-isolate appropriately. You will not be punished, and we won't use your name when we notify your contacts.

If you have any questions or concerns about these policies, do not hesitate to email our president.


Social

Our social chairs are hard at work dreaming up creative events (small group in-person and virtual) to bring the club together. Currently planned events can be found on the 2021 social calendar. Make sure you're subscribed to our mailing list and in the club GroupMe to keep up to date with new social events.
