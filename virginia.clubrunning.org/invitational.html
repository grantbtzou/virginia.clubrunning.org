Spring 2024 meet info


Registration for the Spring 2024 Cavalier Invitational is now live! The meet will be held on Saturday, March 30 at Lannigan Field. The meet will begin at 10 AM. Registration for the meet closes Saturday, March 23. For more information, check out the NIRCA meet page or contact our meet coordinators!

Schedule of Events

The meet is on a rolling schedule. All events will be run women first, then men.

  • 5000m

  • 4x800m relay

  • 4x100m relay

  • 1500m

  • 400m

  • 100m

  • 800m

  • 3000m

  • 200m

  • 4x400m Anything But a Baton Relay

  • The High Jump, Long Jump, and Triple Jump Will Also be Offered.
