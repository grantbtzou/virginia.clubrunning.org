fall 2022 meet info

The Cavalier Invitational is UVA Club Running's home XC meet. This year host a men's 8k and a women's 6k. We hope you will come race with us!

Date: September 17, 2022

Location: Panorama Farms (directions)

Cost: $25 per runner, or $500 per team—whichever is less
