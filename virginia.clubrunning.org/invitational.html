meet info

The Cavalier Invitational is UVA Club Running's home meet. We hope you'll come race with us!

date

October 16, 2021

location

Darden Towe Park, Charlottesville, VA

schedule

The women's 6k begins at 9:00am.

The men's 8k begins at 10:00am.

prizes

Prizes are awarded to the top 3 athletes in each race, and the top 3 teams overall.

registration

Runners can be reigstered through NIRCA's website.

cost

Registration costs $20 per runner or $300 per team.

Make checks payable to: UVA Running Club. Payment also available through Venmo. (Contact our treasurer for details.)
