New member FAQ

We can't wait to welcome new members into our ranks, whether you're a first-year, a transfer student, or just new to the club.

Practice

  • We hold "official" practices Monday-Thursday at 5pm. We meet at Nameless Field.

  • At practice, we'll break into pace groups and go for a run or do a workout, depending on the day.

  • Saturdays are typically workout days. We meet in the morning at Nameless.

  • Unofficial, informally organized long runs are typically held on Sunday.

  • Again, practice attendance is not required in any capacity. Just show up, meet some people, run, and have fun.

Meets

  • We typically go to meets every 2-3 weeks. These meets are usually in other college towns nearby. We have a home meet, usually held in October. The National Intercollegiate Running Club Association (NIRCA) hosts a national race, which only a select group of runners will get to compete in. Nationals is in November.

  • Once a schedule is finalized, the meet coordinators will send it out over the mailing list.

Social events

  • The club hosts parties every 2-3 weeks.

  • In the past, we've also organized group fitness classes, trips to the Sunset Series at Carters Mountain Orchard, and pickup sports on Fridays.

  • You can find a list of planned upcoming social events on the social calendar.

Dues

  • Social dues are $20. You have to pay these if you want to attend parties or other social events the club offers.

  • Racing dues are $35. You only have to pay these if you want to participate in NIRCA events.

  • Racing dues can be paid at any time, so there's no pressure to commit until you know you want to race.

  • Club Running is committed to providing opportunities for all students, regardless of financial status. If you are unable to pay dues for any reason, we are more than happy to provide accommodations. Reach out to any member on the exec team for confidential help.
