Fall 2021

We're back, baby

Fall update & new members

TLDR: team practice is back, and it starts Tuesday, August 24. We're looking forward to holding all kinds of social events, so make sure you're on the mailing list and in the GroupMe.

After more than a year's wait, Club Running is excited to return to normal operations, or at least something very close.

Team practice

  • Full-team, in-person practice begins on Tuesday, August 24, 2021.

  • Practice begins at 5pm. It will be held at Nameless Field, near Memorial Gymnasium.

  • Nightly team dinners will be held after practice. You must comply with any masking requirements that are in effect inside of University dining halls.

Social

Check your inbox and the club GroupMe for details about upcoming parties and other social events.

New members

We can't wait to welcome new members into our ranks, whether you're a first-year, a transfer student, or just new to the club. If you want to learn what we're all about, we highly encourage you to come meet some of us at the Fall 2021 Activities Fair.

This year, we'll also be meeting-and-greeting at RecFest, as part of Wahoo Welcome. RecFest takes place outside of the Aquatic & Fitness Center from 6:00PM-8:00PM on Friday, August 20.
